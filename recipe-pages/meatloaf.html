Meatloaf Recipe

This easy Meatloaf recipe is a classic comfort food made with ground beef, breadcrumbs, and a savory glaze. It's simple, hearty, and perfect for family dinners!

Meatloaf is a dish of ground meat mixed with other ingredients and baked in the shape of a loaf. It is a popular comfort food in many countries and can be made with a variety of meats and seasonings.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup ketchup (for glaze)
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar (for glaze)
  • 1 tablespoon mustard (for glaze)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Lightly grease a loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, breadcrumbs, milk, 1/4 cup ketchup, chopped onion, minced garlic, egg, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper. Mix until all ingredients are well combined.
  3. Transfer the meat mixture into the prepared loaf pan and shape it into a loaf.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together 1/2 cup ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard to create the glaze. Spread the glaze evenly over the top of the meatloaf.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour, or until the meatloaf is cooked through and the internal temperature reaches 160°F (70°C).
  6. Remove from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy your delicious meatloaf!
